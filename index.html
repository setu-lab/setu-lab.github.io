SetuLab Github Projects

We welcome contributions to our open-source repositories, developed by the team at the SetuLab.

MARVEL: Metagenomic Analyses and Retrieval of Viral ELements

MARVEL is a tool for recovery of draft phage genomes from whole community shotgun metagenomic sequencing data.

Go to MARVEL repository

vHULK: Viral Host UnveiLing Kit

Phage Host Prediction using high level features and neural networks.

Go to vHULK repository

MAGset: analyze and compare MAGs of known species

MAGset is a tool to analyze genomes obtained from metagenomic data (MAGs) of known species (which have published reference genomes).

Go to MAGset repository

About Us

We appreciate your visit on our Github page. Setulab is the Bioinformatics Laboratory located at the Institute of Chemistry at the University of São Paulo (USP). The lead researcher is Prof. Dr. João Carlos Setubal.

Our group at the University of São Paulo works in a multidisciplinary manner on the analysis and development of new computational tools, aiming to advance the state of the art in computational biology.
